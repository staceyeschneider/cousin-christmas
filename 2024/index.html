Demands for the Mouse King

Calling all sugar plum fairies and toy soldiers! This year, Cousin Christmas is all about games, games, and more games! We'll have takeout on Monday night, cereal and eggs for breakfast, and Nana will smuggle me one of Dad's many hams for dinner on Christmas Eve.

Nutcracker Games of Glory

Tree Trimming

Nutcracker Army will face off with the Mouse King Army decorating your trees! Best looking: 10 Nuts. Fastest: 5 Nuts.

Nut Pong

Christmas Pong with nuts! Tournament Winner: 20 Nuts. 2nd: 5 Nuts.

Nutcracker March

Stiff-legged march and kick a can of nuts through an obstacle course. Fastest: 10 Nuts.

Jingle Nuts

Strap a tissue box filled with nuts to your backside and dance 'til they all fall out! Winner: 20 Nuts.

Nutstacker

Use a candy cane to stack 8 hex nuts into a towering masterpiece. Fastest winner: 10 Nuts.

Sticky Nuts

Roll nuts across the table and try to land them on a line of sticky tape. Each nut stuck: 5 Nuts.
