The Games

The winner of the first annual Golden Christmas Tree will also be able to pick one event that the parents will compete in on Christmas Eve.

Tree Trimming

Girls vs Boys decorating your trees!

  • Best looking – 2 points
  • Fastest – 1 point

Pooping

Carry a single potato in between your legs... drop in bucket. No hands.

  • Winner – 3 points
  • 2nd – 2 points
  • 3rd – 1 point

Spin The Wrap

Blindfolded, spin around a roll of wrapping paper then pin the nose on the reindeer.

  • Winner – 3 points
  • 2nd – 2 points
  • 3rd – 1 point

Antler Toss

Ten tosses each, and the most rings on the antlers wins.

  • Winner – 3 points
  • 2nd – 2 points
  • 3rd – 1 point

Candy Cane Relay

Teams of 2. Pick up a candy cane using another candy cane in your mouth. Pass to partner.

  • Winner – 3 points
  • 2nd – 2 points
  • 3rd – 1 point

Dance Video

Individuals or team must perform a karaoke song and dance video in costume.

  • Funniest – 3 points
  • Best Choreography – 2 points
  • Most Spirit – 1 point
